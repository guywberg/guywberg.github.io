I'm Guy Weissenberg, a PhD student at EPFL, advised by Prof. Alessandro Chiesa and Prof. Michael Kapralov.

I work in theoretical computer science, with a focus on proof systems, coding theory, sublinear algorithms, and property testing.

I received my M.Sc. from the Weizmann Institute of Science, advised by Prof. Zvika Brakerski. Prior to my PhD, I was a researcher at the Weizmann Institute of Science, working with Prof. Irit Dinur on locally testable codes, and a senior researcher at Ingonyama, working on hardware for cryptographic applications. Before that, I was an Algorithms Developer at Mend.
